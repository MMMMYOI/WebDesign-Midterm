Myoi Mina

Welcome to Myoi Mina's Fanspage

Myoi Mina (Japanese: 名 井南 / み ょ う い み な, English: Mina Sharon Myoi; 03.24.1997), stage name Mina (Korean: 미 나, Japanese: ミ ナ), a dual US-Japan female singer and dancer who developed in Korea and is currently a member of the Korean music group TWICE under JYP Entertainment.
Mina was born in San Antonio, Bill County, Texas, USA, grew up in Nishinomiya, Hyogo Prefecture, Japan. In January 2014, Mina went to Korea to become a trainer of JYP Entertainment. In 2015, she was selected as a member of TWICE for participating in the reality talent show "SIXTEEN" and was major debut on October 20 in the same year.
